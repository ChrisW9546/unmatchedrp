# Ban Appeals

Players may appeal if:

* Stated that they may after a period of 30 days (Perm Bans).
* Staff made an error.
* The punishment was disproportionate to the rule break.
* If told to by a higher member of staff (Management or Above)

All Ban appeals are handled **exclusively** through our **application system**. Appeals made through direct messages, Discord chats, @-tagging staff, or other channels will **not be considered**.

Appeals must remain respectful & will be handled by a member of Management or Above.

If people do appeal we must let them know at the very start that making this appeal does not guarantee that the punishment received will be reduced, removed or reversed.

All appeals must be discussed with another member of Management, Head of Staff or where needed Co-Owner or Owner before actioning.
