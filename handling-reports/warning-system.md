# Punishment Stages

## Stage 1

Educational Warning ( Logged in notes on Cosmo)

## Stage 2

Official Warning (Logged as a Warning on Cosmo, Notes added & Mod Proof Completed)

## Stage 3

2 Day Ban (Logged as a Ban on Cosmo, Notes added, Mod Proof Completed & Banned Role Applied)

## Stage 4

7 Day Ban (Logged as a Ban on Cosmo, Notes added, Mod Proof Completed & Banned Role Applied)

## Stage 5

14 Day - Perm Ban (Authorized by Management) (Logged as a Ban on Cosmo, Notes added, Mod Proof Completed, Banned Role Applied & If necessary Remove from Discord)
