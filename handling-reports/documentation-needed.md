# Documentation Needed

Staff Documentation Needed on ALL Educationals, Warnings & Bans.

The required information is needed for the docuemntation -

* Player Name
* Date & Time
* Rule Broken
* Staff member Handling the Report
* Summary of the Incident
* Summary of the Conversation had



Educational Warning -

* Staff must document what has been said either in a ticket or VC.
* Documentation of these interactions must be put in the notes section of Cosmo.
* Mod Proof Completed with Summary of incident, Proof of Incident, Cosmo Profile Link & Summary of the Conversation had



Official Warnings -

* Staff must document what has been said either in a ticket or VC.
* Documentation of these interactions must be put in the notes section of Cosmo.
* Warning must be placed on their Cosmo Profile & told they will see it when they next fly in.
* Mod Proof Completed with Summary of incident, Proof of Incident, Cosmo Profile Link & Summary of the Conversation had

2 Day Bans -

* Staff must document what has been said either in a ticket or VC.
* Documentation of these interactions must be put in the notes section of Cosmo.
* Ban must be placed on their Cosmo Profile & Banned Role Applied in Discord.
* Mod Proof Completed with Summary of incident, Proof of Incident, Cosmo Profile Link & Summary of the Conversation had

7 Day Bans -

* Staff must get approval from Management for a 7 Day Ban.
* Staff must document what has been said either in a ticket or VC.
* Documentation of these interactions must be put in the notes section of Cosmo.
* Ban must be placed on their Cosmo Profile & Banned Role Applied in Discord.
* Mod Proof Completed with Summary of incident, Proof of Incident, Cosmo Profile Link & Summary of the Conversation had

14 Day Ban - Perm Ban -

* Staff must ask Management to Review then if Management agree proceed.
* Staff must document what has been said either in a ticket or VC.
* Documentation of these interactions must be put in the notes section of Cosmo.
* Ban must be placed on their Cosmo Profile & Banned Role Applied in Discord.
* Mod Proof Completed with Summary of incident, Proof of Incident, Cosmo Profile Link & Summary of the Conversation had
* If Perm Ban is Agreed & Approved please ask your Manager to remove the Player from the discord if it deemed neccessary.



All Documentation must be completed.&#x20;

If it is not it makes other peoples lives difficult to deal with reports or tickets that come through.

If it is found that you consistently fail to follow the document procedure your manager will be in touch.
