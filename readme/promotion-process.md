# Promotion Process

Promotions are based upon:

* Availability of Positions
* Activity
* Professionalism
* Knowledge
* Leadership Qualities
* Reliability

Promotions are earned and can and will be given to those who deserve it, not those who ask for it.

Promotions are not just based on what position you hold to be next in line they are based on many factors including but not limited to those above in the bullet points.

Decisions on Promotions will be decided by Management, Head of Staff & if needed Co-Owner / Owner.&#x20;

Any negativity or toxicity to someone else being promoted will be dealt with quickly and effectively.&#x20;
