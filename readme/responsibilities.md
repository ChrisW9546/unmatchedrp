# Staff Responsibilities

As Staff within Unmatched RP you have responsibilites to the Community & The Server.

Some of the Responsibilities are listed below -

* Respond to reports.
* Monitor RP quality.
* Enforce server rules.
* Assist new players.
* Support community events.
* Maintain professionalism.
* Community Engagement.
* Follow proper Procedures.
* Remain Unbiased at All Times.
* Maintain a Higher Standard of RP.
