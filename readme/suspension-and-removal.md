# Suspension & Removal

Unmatched RP respects and trusts all of our staff members to be able to help towards the server & community. However there may be times where we have to question this if things happen. Due to this as with any server we have the right to suspend or remove you depending on the circumstances.

Staff may be suspended while an investigation is underway. If you are suspended you will be informed through out the process to keep you up to date of how the investigation is going. With this being said as with any investigation we cannot and will not give you the full details of any report, information provided or the person/people that have reported things. Once and investigation is concluded you will be invited to join a review so we can discuss the next steps.

Removal may occur for:

* Gross misconduct
* Abuse of permissions
* Repeated policy breaches
* Inactivity
* Loss of trust
* Breach of NDA
* Toxicity

Management and Above hold the right to remove staff at any time if there is reason to do so. If this happens you will be informed as to why but will also have your say and chance to give feedback.
