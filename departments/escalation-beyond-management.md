# Escalation Beyond Management

In the event that an issue has been raised to Management but they need to take it further only then can issues be raised above to the following -&#x20;



* **Assistant Head of Staff** - Bobby
* **Head of Staff** - Kizza
* **Co-Owner** - Joker
* **Owner** - Mac

The Process must be followed of Management First, then Assistant Head of Staff, then Head of Staff, then Co-Owner and ultimately after that Owner.

There may be circumstances that superseed this and escalation has to go from Management straight to Ownership but this will be determined by a number of factors that will be decided by Management at the time.
