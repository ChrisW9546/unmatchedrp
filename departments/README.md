# Departments

**Moderation Team** - Handles Player Reports, Rule breaks,  general player help, questions, and assists with resolving minor issues.

**Player Experience Team** - Manages Whitelist Applications, Filling out Feedback Forms, Going through player suggestions and player retention ideas.

&#x20;**Events Team** - Plans, hosts, and manages server-wide events, keeping the community active and engaged.&#x20;

**Emergency Service Team** - Supports the Police, UHS, Fire, and Highways — ensuring quality RP, training, and standards.&#x20;

**Purchase Team** - Manages donations, Tebex, priority queues, and any purchase-related player support.&#x20;

**Business Team** - Oversees player-owned businesses, applications, approvals, and business performance.&#x20;

**Development Team** - Works on scripts, fixes, server updates, optimisation, and overall tech improvements.&#x20;

**Gang Team** - Responsible for overseeing all gang activity, ensuring high-quality criminal RP, handling gang applications, maintaining standards, and supporting gang story development.&#x20;

**Social & Media Team** - Manages Unmatched RP’s social presence, supports content creators, promotes community engagement, oversees announcements, and maintains a positive public image.
